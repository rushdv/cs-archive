# Resources for Physics I (PHY 1201)

## Syllabus Topics
- Heat & Thermodynamics: Temperature, Kinetic Theory, Laws of Thermodynamics.
- Properties of Matter: Stress, Strain, Elasticity, Viscosity.
- Waves & Oscillations: Simple Harmonic Motion, Doppler Effect.

## Recommended Books
- **Concepts of Physics** by H.C. Verma
- **Fundamentals of Physics** by Halliday, Resnick
