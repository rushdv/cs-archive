# Resources for Mathematics I (MATH 1101)

## Syllabus Topics
- Differential Calculus: Limits, Continuity, Differentiation, Maxima/Minima.
- Integral Calculus: Definite/Indefinite Integrals, Area under curves.

## Recommended Books
- **Calculus** by Howard Anton
- **Calculus and Analytic Geometry** by George B. Thomas
