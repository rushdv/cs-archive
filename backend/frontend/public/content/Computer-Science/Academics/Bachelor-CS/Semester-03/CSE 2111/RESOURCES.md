# Resources for Data Structure (CSE 2111)

## Syllabus Topics
- Arrays, Linked Lists (Singly, Doubly, Circular)
- Stacks & Queues (Implementation & Applications)
- Recursion
- Trees (Binary Trees, BST, AVL)
- Graphs (Representation, BFS, DFS)
- Sorting (Bubble, Selection, Insertion, Merge, Quick) & Searching

## Recommended Books
- **Data Structures and Algorithms in C** by Reema Thareja
- **Fundamentals of Data Structures in C** by Ellis Horowitz
