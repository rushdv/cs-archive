# Heat & Thermodynamics

## Fundamentals
- **Heat (Q):** Energy transfer due to temp difference.
- **Temperature (T):** Measure of thermal energy.

## Laws of Thermodynamics
1.  **Zeroth Law:** Thermal Equilibrium principle.
2.  **First Law:** Conservation of Energy (ΔQ = ΔU + ΔW).
3.  **Second Law:** Entropy increases; Heat flows hot → cold. Efficiency < 100%.

## Processes
- **Isothermal:** Constant Temp (ΔT=0).
- **Adiabatic:** No Heat Exchange (ΔQ=0).
- **Isobaric:** Constant Pressure.
- **Isochoric:** Constant Volume.
