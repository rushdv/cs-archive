# AC Circuits

## Alternating Current
Current that changes direction periodically.
- **Frequency (f):** Cycles per second (Hz).
- **Period (T):** Time for one cycle (T = 1/f).

## Phasors
Representation of sinusoidal signals using complex numbers.

## Impedance (Z)
Resistance in AC circuits.
- **Resistor:** Z = R
- **Inductor:** Z = jωL (Lags)
- **Capacitor:** Z = 1/jωC (Leads)

## Power in AC
- **Real Power (P):** VI cos(θ) (Watts)
- **Reactive Power (Q):** VI sin(θ) (VAR)
- **Apparent Power (S):** VI (VA)
