# Resources for Electrical Engineering & Circuit Analysis (CSE 1205)

## Syllabus Topics
- DC Circuits (KVL, KCL, Ohm's Law)
- Network Theorems (Thevenin, Norton, Superposition)
- AC Circuits (Phasors, Impedance, Resonance)
- Magnetic Circuits

## Recommended Books
- **Fundamentals of Electric Circuits** by Charles K. Alexander and Matthew N. O. Sadiku
- **Introductory Circuit Analysis** by Robert L. Boylestad
