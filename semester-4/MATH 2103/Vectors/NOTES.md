# Vectors

## Operations
- **Dot Product (Scalar):** A · B = |A||B|cosθ. Result is a number. Example: Work Done.
- **Cross Product (Vector):** A × B = |A||B|sinθ n. Result is a vector perpedicular to both. Example: Torque.

## Vector Calculus
- **Del Operator (∇):** Vector differential operator.
- **Gradient (∇φ):** Scalar → Vector. Direction of max increase.
- **Divergence (∇·V):** Vector → Scalar. Efficiency of a source/sink.
- **Curl (∇×V):** Vector → Vector. Rotation of a field.
