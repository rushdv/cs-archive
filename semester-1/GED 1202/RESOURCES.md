# Resources for Emergence of Bangladesh (GED 1202)

## Course Overview
History of Bangladesh's independence and its emergence as a sovereign nation.

## Topics
- Language Movement (1952)
- Six Point Movement (1966)
- Mass Uprising (1969)
- Liberation War (1971)
- Constitution of Bangladesh
